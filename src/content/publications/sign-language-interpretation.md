Survey and comparison of recent ML architectures for sign language interpretation, including CNN, LSTM, landmark-based methods, Transformer and hybrids, with evaluations on static and dynamic gesture datasets. Includes our real-time ISL system using MediaPipe landmark extraction and LSTM-based temporal modeling.
